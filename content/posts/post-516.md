## The App Store on Apple Vision Pro expands to new markets

June 18, 2024

Apple Vision Pro will launch in China mainland, Hong Kong, Japan, and Singapore on **June 28** and in Australia, Canada, France, Germany, and the United Kingdom on **July 12**. Your apps and games will be automatically available on the App Store in regions you’ve selected in App Store Connect.

If you’d like, you can:

- Manage the availability of your [visionOS apps](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-your-apps-availability/manage-availability-for-your-app-on-the-app-store)and[compatible iPhone or iPad apps](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-your-apps-availability/manage-availability-of-iphone-and-ipad-apps-on-apple-vision-pro)at any time.
- Request to have your app [evaluated directly on Apple Vision Pro](https://developer.apple.com/visionos/compatibility-evaluations/).
- [Localize your product page metadata](https://developer.apple.com/localization/)for local audiences.

You can also learn how to [build native apps](https://developer.apple.com/visionos/pathway/) to fully take advantage of exciting visionOS features.
